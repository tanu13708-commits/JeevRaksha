Welcome to JeevRaksha

Your trusted platform for reporting and rescuing animals in need. We connect caring citizens with NGOs to ensure every animal gets timely help.

Why JeevRaksha?

Every day, countless animals face danger, injury, or abandonment. JeevRaksha makes it easy for you to report these cases and get help fast. Our platform is designed for quick action, clear communication, and real impact.

  • Easy Reporting: Share details and location of animals in distress in just a few clicks.
  • Instant NGO Connection: Your report reaches rescue teams and NGOs immediately.
  • Real Results: Track rescue status and see the difference you make.

Join us in building a safer, kinder world for animals. Your action matters!

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about JeevRaksha

How do I report an injured animal?

Click on "Report Animal" in the menu, fill in the details about the animal's condition, upload a photo, and share the location. Our rescue team will be notified immediately!

Is uploading a photo mandatory?

Yes, a photo helps our rescue team assess the situation quickly and prepare the right equipment. It also helps verify the report and locate the animal faster.

What if I don't know the exact location?

Use the "Use My Location" button to automatically share your GPS coordinates. You can also describe nearby landmarks like shops, temples, or building names.

How long does rescue take?

Our average response time is 12-15 minutes in urban areas. For critical cases, we prioritize immediate dispatch. You can track the rescue live on our "Live Tracking" page.

What happens after rescue?

The animal is taken to our partner vet clinic for treatment. We provide regular updates on the animal's condition. Once recovered, we find them loving forever homes through our adoption program.

How can I track my reported rescue?

After reporting, you'll receive a Report ID (like JR-2026-X7K9). Go to "Live Tracking" and enter this ID to see real-time updates including team dispatch, ETA, and rescue status.

How is my donation used?

Your donation goes towards: Medical care (40%), Food & Shelter (30%), Rescue Operations (20%), and Rehabilitation (10%). We maintain complete transparency with quarterly reports.

Will I get a tax receipt?

Yes! All donations are eligible for tax benefits under Section 80G. You'll receive an automated receipt via email within 24 hours of your donation.

Can I set up monthly donations?

Absolutely! Recurring donors are our heroes. Contact us at donate@jeevraksha.org to set up monthly auto-debit. You'll receive a special "Guardian Angel" badge on your profile!

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers! You can help with rescues, foster animals, assist at vet clinics, or help with awareness campaigns. Email us at volunteer@jeevraksha.org to join our family.

Are you partnered with NGOs?

Yes! We work with 50+ verified animal welfare NGOs across India. Our platform connects citizens directly with nearby rescue organizations for faster response.
